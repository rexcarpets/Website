Casablanca Patterned Carpet


Be bold with home design by introducing the vibrant textured finishes of our value Casablanca collection into your rooms. Perfect for those looking to create an impact on a budget, the Casablanca Textured Carpet is available in four striking patterns.

Crafted from 100% Polypropylene, this easy-clean carpet resists stains naturally making it a popular choice for busy areas of the home. This family friendly textured carpet has a hard wearing surface that is backed with felt to help extend the life of the carpet.

Super Top Twist® Carpet


The Super Top Twist Plain Carpet is a best seller and for good reason. Perfect for busy family homes, this hard wearing carpet has a tufted twist surface which offers a luxurious feel underfoot and is packed with technology to make cleaning and maintain your carpet straightforward.

Stainguard XL superior fibres allow this modern carpet to maintain its new appearance for longer. ActionBac is also included within this carpet’s construction. This all synthetic, stretchable secondary backing fabric resists stains and moisture well, extending the life of your carpet.

Boasting an impressive 20 year no fuss stain warranty, this heather twist carpet is the perfect flooring solution for many areas of a busy home as it combines durability and easy maintenance with super sleek styling.

Viceroy® Twist Carpet


Bring your home up to date with the contemporary Viceroy carpet. A Carpetright best seller, the Viceroy combines good looks and cosy underfoot comfort with practical performance; making it an ideal choice for busy spaces within the home.

Available in beautiful, neutral heather tones, the Viceroy has been designed to complement a variety of colour schemes. Tough polypropylene fibres are used within this twist carpet, making it a wise investment for areas that experience heavy foot traffic such as stairs and hallways. What’s more, this hard wearing carpet is virtually stain-proof and comes with a 15 year stain warranty to give you peace of mind.

Kosset Two Step Plus® Carpet


Packed with stain resistant innovations including Aqua Pro Tec, our Two Step Plus Twist Heather Carpet is a sensational carpet that has become a firm favourite within busy family homes. Boasting a lifetime stain warranty, this virtually stain proof carpet is available in a vibrant range of rich colours.

Ideal as a bedroom carpet or lounge carpet, this textured heather carpet uses a fibre mix to create a flecked, toned effect that adds depth to any room. The Two Step Plus incorporates Aqua Pro Tech, a pioneering carpet technology that prevents hot, cold or fizzy liquid spills soaking instantly into the carpet. It uses a unique barrier to keep liquids on the carpet’s surface for longer, giving you more time to clean it up with a paper towel.

Hever Castle® Saxony Carpet


Update your home with our exclusive Hever Castle collection. A popular choice for the bedroom, this high quality Saxony carpet has an incredibly dense woven pile which delivers superior underfoot comfort and has a soft plush appearance.

This heavy weight Hever Castle carpet is available in multiple widths for a seamless finish even in bigger rooms and comes with a 20 year stain guarantee for peace of mind. It is crafted from superior multi-strand Stainguard XL fibres which maintain this carpet’s new appearance for longer.

This sumptuously deep carpet has a thick pile which provides excellent insulation and a luxurious texture underfoot. Order your free carpet samples today to experience this exclusive carpet or take advantage of our free home consultation which can be booked quickly and easily online.

Cardinal Twist Carpet


The Cardinal Twist Plain Carpet is a popular, classic style which has deep pile that delivers superior underfoot comfort. Available in an elegant range of neutral colours, this plain carpet will bring rustic tones to your current decor.

This elegant twist plain carpet is suitable as a bedroom carpet, dining room carpet and lounge carpet, or as a flooring solution for your stairs and landing. The Cardinal Carpet’s Polypropylene fibre construction is surprisingly resilient, making it ideal for busy areas of the home.

A favourite among families because of the combination of comfort and durability, this Cardinal Carpet is available in 4 and 5 metre widths to create a seamless finish even in large spaces.
